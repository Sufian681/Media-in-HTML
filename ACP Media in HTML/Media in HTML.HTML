My School

The name of my school is IISJ

India International School in Japan (IISJ) is an esteemed educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to the Indian diaspora in Japan. Founded with the vision of fostering a strong sense of cultural identity and academic excellence, the school offers a curriculum that aligns with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) of India. IISJ emphasizes a well-rounded education, integrating academics with extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and cultural events, allowing students to explore their interests and talents. The school prides itself on a diverse and inclusive environment, catering to students from various backgrounds while promoting Indian culture and values. Highly qualified educators work diligently to ensure that students receive personalized attention, encouraging critical thinking and creativity. The school's modern facilities, including science and computer laboratories, libraries, and sports infrastructure, create an enriching learning atmosphere. Moreover, IISJ actively engages with parents and the broader community, organizing events and programs that foster collaboration and connection. Through its commitment to excellence and holistic development, India International School in Japan prepares students to thrive in an increasingly globalized world while remaining rooted in their cultural heritage.
